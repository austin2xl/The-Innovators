      About Cross NCR

      Cross NCR is a comprehensive system designed to streamline the management and reporting of Non-Conformance Reports (NCRs). This tool helps businesses to efficiently handle issues with suppliers, manage corrective actions, and maintain compliance standards. With features like real-time reporting, status tracking, and integration with external systems, Cross NCR ensures that non-conformance issues are addressed promptly and accurately.
