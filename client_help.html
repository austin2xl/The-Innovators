    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I create a new NCR? +

    To create a new NCR, navigate to the 'Create NCR ' page and fill out the required fields. Ensure all mandatory information is provided before submitting the form.

    How can I filter reports? +

    Go to the 'REPORTS' page, and use the filter options available at the top of the page. You can filter by status, priority, and date.

    Can I download reports? +

    Yes, reports can be downloaded as PDFs. Use the 'Generate PDF' button on the 'REPORTS' page after selecting the desired options.
