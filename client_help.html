      About Cross NCR

      Cross NCR is a comprehensive system designed to streamline the management and reporting of Non-Conformance Reports (NCRs). This tool helps businesses to efficiently handle issues with suppliers, manage corrective actions, and maintain compliance standards. With features like real-time reporting, status tracking, and integration with external systems, Cross NCR ensures that non-conformance issues are addressed promptly and accurately.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      • What is the NCR system?

        The NCR (Non-Conformance Report) system helps businesses report, track, and resolve issues related to products or suppliers that don't meet quality standards.

      • How can I create an NCR?

        To create an NCR, log in to your account and go to the"Create NCR" page. Fill in the necessary information and submit the form.

      • Can I track NCR status?

        Yes, NCR status can be tracked via the NCR Reports. The status is updated as the issue progresses.

      • How do I escalate an NCR issue?

        If an NCR issue requires escalation, contact your system administrator for guidance on the next steps.

      • Can I upload documents for an NCR?

        Yes, documents such as images or reports can be attached when creating or editing an NCR.

        To request a quote, please fill out the contact form below with your details, including any additional media that may assist in your request.
